About the Company

Founded by Ibukunoluwa (IBK) Adebayo, Greensol leverages Africa's natural resources and modern technology to provide clean, reliable energy and utilities. Our managed marketplace offers sustainable solutions for electricity, water, gas, and internet, using advanced technologies like GIS and AR. Our mission is to make reliable utilities standard for all, promoting a greener and more sustainable future.



Job Description

  • Develop UI mockups and prototypes that clearly illustrate how sites function and look.
  • Conduct user research and gather feedback to inform design decisions.
  • Collaborate with developers to ensure accurate implementation of designs.
  • Maintain design documentation and create design systems to ensure consistency across products.

Requirements

  • Proficiency in design tools such as Sketch, Adobe XD, Figma, and Adobe Creative Suite.
  • Familiarity with front-end development technologies (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) is a plus.
  • Bachelor's degree in Design, Computer Science, or a related field.
  • Ability to create wireframes, mockups, and high-fidelity prototypes.
  • Ability to manage multiple projects and meet deadlines.
  • Ability to work collaboratively with developers, product managers, and other stakeholders.
